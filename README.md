# Sales Performance & Demand Forecasting
📊 Sales Performance & Demand Forecasting Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to identify revenue drivers, profit leakage, and demand trends using SQL, Python, and Power BI. The dashboard provides business insights on regional sales performance, product profitability, and customer segments, along with time-series forecasting to predict future demand.

The goal of this project is to simulate how a Data Analyst supports decision-making in an e-commerce or retail organization.

🎯 Business Objectives

Analyze sales performance across regions and customer segments

Identify loss-making products and discount impact on profitability

Monitor monthly revenue trends

Forecast future sales demand

Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for decision-makers


📈 Dashboard Features
1️⃣ Executive Overview

Provides high-level business performance indicators.

Visuals include:

KPI Cards (Sales, Profit, Orders)

Sales by Region

Sales by Customer Segment

Monthly Revenue Trend
